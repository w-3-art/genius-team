Market Gaps (Opportunities)

AI-Native Workflow Automation HIGH
No tool offers AI that drafts proposals from calls, auto-categorizes expenses, or predicts timelines from historical data.
Solo-First Design HIGH
Most tools are team-first, adapted for solo users. Room for ground-up solo operator design with zero onboarding friction.
Integrated Financial Intelligence MEDIUM
No tool combines PM + invoicing + tax estimation + profit tracking in a single view.
Premium Client Experience MEDIUM
Client portals exist but are basic. Progress dashboards, automated updates, and self-service could differentiate.
Community & Marketplace LOW
No PM tool connects freelancers for referrals or subcontracting. Built-in network effects could create a moat.
