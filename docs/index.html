Our philosophy

The opposite of a black box.

We don't want to replace you. We want to make you capable.

What we avoid
  • Code thrown over the wall with zero explanation.
  • Magic tools that break silently when you need them.
  • One-shot generators that leave you stuck on iteration 2.
  • Lock-in ecosystems that own your work.
What we stand for
  • A team that teaches while it works with you.
  • Transparent artifacts — you see every step, every decision.
  • A review loop so you improve every cycle.
  • Local-first, open source by default, your data stays yours.
