Describe your idea. Genius Team assigns the right specialists to guide you — design, code, test, deploy. You learn and build at the same time.

Describe your idea

Tell the interviewer what you want to build. It asks smart questions to understand your vision — no technical knowledge required.

→ Produces: Discovery brief, target audience, core features
STEP 02

Get your blueprint

Specs, market analysis, and architecture — your AI crew researches and plans everything alongside you.

→ Produces: Specifications, market report, design system

Specialized agents write code while you review every decision. Frontend, backend, database — each has its own expert.

→ Each PR reviewed by QA + security before merge
