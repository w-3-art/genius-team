How it works

You lead. Agents follow.

Four steps from idea to production. You're the boss at every stage.

Step 1 / 4

Share your vision

The interviewer agent asks smart questions — your market, your users, your goals. No forms, no templates. Just a conversation that shapes a real plan.

Step 2 / 4

Review the blueprint

Specs, design system, architecture — each specialist proposes, you approve. Nothing is decided without your explicit OK. Three human checkpoints ensure alignment.

Step 3 / 4

Build together

Dev agents write code, QA agents test it, and you see every change in real time. Vibe coding — creative flow with AI support. You learn while shipping.

Step 4 / 4

Go live with confidence

Security scan, performance check, accessibility audit. Then one-click deploy. Your app is live — and you understand every line behind it.
