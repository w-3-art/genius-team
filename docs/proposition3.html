Common questions

Good questions deserve clear answers.

Do I need to know how to code?+

No. That's the whole point. Genius Team is designed for people who want to learn to build with AI. The 42 agents guide you through every step — but you'll actually learn real skills along the way.

Is it really free?+

Yes. Genius Team is MIT-licensed open source. You need an API key from your AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI) — their pricing applies, but Genius Team itself is free forever.

What can I build with it?+

Anything you can describe. Web apps, mobile apps, APIs, landing pages, tools, dashboards. The agents handle architecture, design, frontend, backend, testing, deployment — and teach you along the way.

How is this different from ChatGPT / Cursor / Bolt?+

Those are single tools. Genius Team is a full team — 42 specialized agents that collaborate across the entire product lifecycle. It's not one AI answering questions, it's a structured workflow: PM → Designer → Developer → QA → DevOps, with handoffs and quality gates.

Does the AI do everything for me?+

No — and that's a feature, not a bug. You stay in the driver's seat. The agents guide, suggest, and build alongside you, but you make the decisions. This means you actually learn and can maintain what you build.
